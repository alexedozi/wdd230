Our Vision

The XMME CHAMBER OF COMMERCE is a subscription-based association dedicated to creating an eco-system that provides access to markets, financial inclusion and socio-economic opportunities to the African, Small, Micro, Medium Enterprises. We are a collective voice for small businesses. We are providing an e-commerce platform to address the needs of consumer and entreprenuers to resolve challenges of market access, inter member trade and global opporunity creation. We have over 10 000 members that are based on our district development model.
